About Tanza Public Market

Tanza Public Market is committed to providing a transparent and fair marketplace for all. Our price monitoring system ensures that both vendors and consumers have access to real-time pricing information, promoting fair trade practices and market transparency.

With our digital platform, we bridge the gap between traditional market practices and modern technology, creating a more efficient and trustworthy marketplace for everyone.
